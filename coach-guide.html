GYM TIMER — COACH QUICK START

Connect your phone, pick a workout, and go.

1 Start the Timer

  1. Make sure the laptop is on and the TV is showing the timer screen.
  2. If not running, double-click the "Gym Timer" icon on the desktop.
  3. Click anywhere on the screen to go fullscreen. Press ESC to exit.

2 Connect Your Phone

  1. Make sure your phone is on the same WiFi network as the laptop.
  2. Scan the QR code on the TV with your phone camera.
  3. Enter the coach password: coach123
  4. The QR screen will dismiss and the timer is ready. Sound works automatically.

3 Choose a Timer Mode

Tap a mode on your phone, configure time/rounds, then tap Start.

4 Screen Colors

Orange = Prep Green = Work Red = Rest / Final 3s

Display pulses red during the final 3 seconds. "TIME!" is announced on completion.

5 Controls

From your phone:

  • Play / Pause — main control buttons
  • Reset — restart the current timer
  • Stop — end the workout, return to clock

From the laptop keyboard:

  • Space Play / Pause
  • R Reset    S Stop    F11 Toggle fullscreen

6 Settings (on phone)

Tap Settings at the bottom of the controller:

  • Brightness — screen brightness (1–5)
  • Volume — beep volume (0–5, 0 = mute)
  • 12/24hr — toggle clock format
  • Prep Countdown — 10-second lead-in before workout
  • Test Sound — verify audio is working

7 Troubleshooting

  • QR won't scan? — Type the URL shown below the QR code into your phone browser.
  • No sound? — Make sure the timer was launched from the desktop icon. Check volume is above 0.
  • "DISCONNECTED"? — Refresh the browser. Your phone will auto-reconnect.
  • Phone disconnected? — It reconnects automatically. If not, refresh and re-enter password.
  • Can't exit fullscreen? — Press ESC or F11.
Tip: You can tap number values on the controller (minutes, seconds, rounds) to type them directly instead of using the +/- buttons.
